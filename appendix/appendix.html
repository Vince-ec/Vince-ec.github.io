Interactive Appendix

Here we present an interative appendix for Estrada-Carpenter et al. 2020. Clicking on the points will open an interative morphology and SFH plot. Hovering over a point will bring up the galaxies properites (appropriate to the plot). Using the lasso tool allows for selection of multiple objects which will be highlighted on all plots. We also provide a figure with all star-formation histories and (3"X3")? cutouts.
