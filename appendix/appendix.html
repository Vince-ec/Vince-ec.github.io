Interactive Appendix

Here we present an interactive appendix for Estrada-Carpenter et al. 2020. The plots in the figure below shows the relationship between formation redshift (z50) - log stellar surface mass density within 1 kiloparsec (log(Σ1)) [top left], age (t50) - log specific star-formation rate (log(sSFR)) [top right], quenching timescale (tq) - redshift (zgrism) [bottom left], and size (Reff) - log mass (log(M/M☉)) [bottom right].

  • At the top right of each of the interactive plots there are several buttons that allow you to interact with the figures.
    • These include (in order) tools to pan the figure, zoom in, select multiple points (lasso tool), scroll zoom, click on points (tap tool), and a tool to refresh the plots
  • Points selected with the lasso tool will be highlighted on all plots.
  • Hovering over a point will bring up the galaxy’s properties (appropriate to the plot).
  • Clicking on the points will open a bio page including a morphology, interactive star-formation history, and interactive spectrum plot (with best fit spectrum).
  • The interactive bio pages include the ability to pan and zoom.

We also provide a figure with all star-formation histories and (4"X4") cutouts, as well as text versions of Table 1 and Table 2 .
